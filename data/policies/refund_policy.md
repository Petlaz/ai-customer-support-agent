# Refund Policy

**Nexus Software — Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

---

## 1. Money-Back Guarantee

All new customers are eligible for a full refund within **30 days** of their initial purchase. No questions asked. To request a refund within the 30-day window, contact support with your account email and order confirmation.

## 2. Monthly Subscriptions

- Monthly subscription fees are **non-refundable** once the billing cycle has started.
- Exception: billing errors, duplicate charges, or service outages exceeding 24 continuous hours qualify for a prorated refund regardless of billing date.
- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged for the following month.

## 3. Annual Subscriptions

- Annual plans may receive a **prorated refund** for unused months if cancelled within the first 6 months.
- Cancellations after 6 months are not eligible for a refund on the remaining term.
- Exception: if Nexus Software materially changes or discontinues a feature you purchased specifically for, you may request a full prorated refund regardless of timeline.

## 4. Duplicate Charges

- If a customer is charged more than once for the same billing period, the duplicate charge will be refunded in full within **5–7 business days**.
- Customers must provide the transaction IDs for both charges when reporting a duplicate.

## 5. Charges After Cancellation

- If a customer is charged after a confirmed cancellation, the charge will be refunded in full.
- Cancellation confirmation is sent via email. Customers should retain this email as proof.

## 6. High-Value Refunds

- Refund requests exceeding **$500** require approval from a senior support manager.
- These requests are escalated automatically and reviewed within 2 business days.
- The customer will be notified of the decision by email.

## 7. Refund Processing Time

- Approved refunds are processed within **5–7 business days**.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.
- Bank processing times may add an additional 3–5 business days depending on the financial institution.

## 8. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:
- Add-on purchases (integrations, storage upgrades) after 7 days of purchase
- Custom onboarding or professional services fees
- Fees for accounts terminated due to Terms of Service violations

## 9. How to Request a Refund

1. Contact support at support@nexussoftware.com or via the in-app chat
2. Include your account email, order/invoice number, and reason for the refund
3. For duplicate charges, include both transaction IDs
4. Our team will respond within 1 business day
