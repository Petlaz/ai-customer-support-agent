# Billing Policy

**Nexus Software — Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

---

## 1. Billing Cycles

- **Monthly plans:** Billed on the same date each month (the date of the original subscription).
- **Annual plans:** Billed once per year on the subscription anniversary date.
- If a billing date falls on a weekend or public holiday, the charge is processed on the next business day.

## 2. Accepted Payment Methods

- Credit cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- Debit cards with a Visa or Mastercard logo
- PayPal
- ACH bank transfer (annual plans only, US customers)
- Purchase orders (Enterprise plans only)

## 3. Pricing Plans

| Plan | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Team Members |
|------|--------------|-------------|-------------|
| Starter | $19/month | $190/year | Up to 3 |
| Pro | $49/month | $490/year | Up to 15 |
| Business | $99/month | $990/year | Up to 50 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Unlimited |

All prices are in USD and exclude applicable taxes.

## 4. Plan Upgrades and Downgrades

- **Upgrades:** Take effect immediately. You are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle.
- **Downgrades:** Take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. No refund is issued for the current cycle.
- Downgrading may result in loss of access to features or data that exceed the new plan's limits.

## 5. Failed Payments

- If a payment fails, Nexus Software will retry the charge **3 times** over 7 days.
- After 3 failed attempts, the account is suspended (read-only access).
- The customer is notified by email at each retry and upon suspension.
- The account is reactivated immediately upon successful payment.
- Accounts suspended for more than **30 days** due to non-payment may be permanently deleted.

## 6. Taxes

- Nexus Software collects applicable sales tax for customers in US states where required.
- EU customers are charged VAT at the applicable rate for their country.
- Tax-exempt organizations must provide a valid exemption certificate before purchase.

## 7. Invoices

- Invoices are generated automatically at each billing event.
- Invoices are available in the account dashboard under **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**.
- Invoices can be downloaded as PDF or emailed to a billing email address.
- Custom billing details (company name, address, VAT number) can be configured in billing settings.

## 8. Billing Disputes

- Billing disputes must be raised within **60 days** of the charge date.
- To dispute a charge, contact support@nexussoftware.com with the invoice number and a description of the issue.
- Disputes are investigated and resolved within 5 business days.
- Chargebacks filed with a bank without first contacting support may result in account suspension.

## 9. Subscription Cancellation

- Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time from **Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription**.
- A cancellation confirmation email is sent immediately.
- Access continues until the end of the paid billing period.
- Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, after which it is permanently deleted.
