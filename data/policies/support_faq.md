# Support FAQ

**Nexus Software — Frequently Asked Questions**

---

## Account & Access

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Go to the login page and click "Forgot password". Enter your account email address and we will send a password reset link within 2 minutes. The link expires after 1 hour. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

**Q: My account is locked. What do I do?**
A: Accounts are locked after 5 consecutive failed login attempts as a security measure. Wait 15 minutes and try again, or use the password reset flow to unlock immediately. If the issue persists, contact support and we can manually unlock the account after identity verification.

**Q: How do I add team members to my account?**
A: Go to **Settings → Team → Invite Members**. Enter the email addresses of the people you want to invite. They will receive an invitation email with a link to create their account. The number of seats available depends on your plan.

**Q: Can I change my account email address?**
A: Yes. Go to **Settings → Profile → Email Address**. You will be asked to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: To delete your account, go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account** and follow the confirmation steps. All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. This action cannot be undone. For GDPR data deletion requests, contact support@nexussoftware.com.

## Billing & Subscriptions

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, and ACH bank transfers (annual plans, US only). Enterprise customers can pay by purchase order.

**Q: Where can I find my invoices?**
A: Invoices are available in **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**. Each invoice can be downloaded as a PDF.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Go to **Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription**. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period and you will not be charged again. A confirmation email is sent immediately.

**Q: Will I get a refund if I cancel?**
A: Monthly subscriptions are not refunded once the billing cycle has started. Annual subscriptions may receive a prorated refund if cancelled within the first 6 months. New customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase. See the full Refund Policy for details.

**Q: Why was I charged after I cancelled?**
A: If you were charged after receiving a cancellation confirmation email, this is a billing error. Contact support immediately with your cancellation confirmation and we will issue a full refund.

## Product Features

**Q: Does Nexus Software support Single Sign-On (SSO)?**
A: Yes. SSO via SAML 2.0 is available on the Business and Enterprise plans. Supported identity providers include Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, and OneLogin. Configuration instructions are available in our SSO Setup Guide in the Help Center.

**Q: How do I export my data?**
A: You can export your data from **Settings → Data → Export**. Available formats are CSV and JSON. Large exports are processed in the background and emailed to your account address when ready, typically within 15 minutes.

**Q: How many team members can I have on the Pro plan?**
A: The Pro plan supports up to 15 team members. For more seats, upgrade to the Business plan (50 seats) or Enterprise (unlimited).

**Q: What is your uptime SLA?**
A: We guarantee 99.9% monthly uptime for Pro and Business plans, and 99.95% for Enterprise plans. In the event of downtime exceeding the SLA, customers are eligible for service credits. See our Status Page at status.nexussoftware.com for real-time information.

**Q: Does Nexus Software have a mobile app?**
A: Yes. Native apps are available for iOS (iOS 15+) and Android (Android 10+). Download from the App Store or Google Play. The mobile app supports all core features except advanced admin settings, which require the web app.

**Q: Can I integrate Nexus Software with Salesforce?**
A: Yes. A native Salesforce integration is available on the Business and Enterprise plans. Install it from **Settings → Integrations → Salesforce**. API access is also available on Pro and above for custom integrations.

## Technical Issues

**Q: The app is slow or not loading. What should I do?**
A: First, clear your browser cache and cookies and reload the page. Check our status page at status.nexussoftware.com for any ongoing incidents. Try a different browser. If the issue persists, contact support with your browser version and a screenshot of any error messages.

**Q: I'm getting an error when uploading files. What's the limit?**
A: The maximum file upload size is 100 MB per file. Supported formats depend on the feature — for document uploads, we support PDF, DOCX, XLSX, CSV, and PNG/JPG. If you are within these limits and still seeing errors, contact support.

**Q: The API is returning 401 Unauthorized. What should I check?**
A: Check that your API key is correct and has not been revoked. API keys are available in **Settings → API → API Keys**. Ensure the key has the required permissions for the endpoint you are calling. Rate limits are 1,000 requests/minute on Pro and 5,000/minute on Business/Enterprise.
